Why You need a website coded in html.

By Washington Maina

In today's digital age, having a website is essential for any business or individual looking to establish an online presence. However, not all websites are created equal. Websites that are coded using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are considered to be "static" or "dynamic", and they offer several advantages over other types of websites. In this blog post, we will discuss the importance of coded websites and why you should choose to build your website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

First and foremost, coded websites are more flexible and customizable. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, developers can create unique designs and layouts that are tailored to their clients' specific needs. This allows for a more personalized and professional look, which can be a major selling point for businesses.

In addition to design, coded websites are also more interactive and user-friendly. With JavaScript, developers can create interactive elements such as forms, buttons, and animations, which can greatly enhance the user experience. This is especially important for businesses that want to engage with their customers and make it easy for them to interact with their website.

Coded websites are also more responsive and adaptable to different devices. With HTML and CSS, developers can create responsive designs that automatically adjust to the screen size of the device being used, whether it's a desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. This ensures that your website will look and function correctly on any device, providing a seamless experience for your users.

Another important aspect of coded websites is Search Engine Optimization (SEO). HTML, CSS and JavaScript allows for better and easier optimization for search engines like Google. With the help of proper tags and meta descriptions, it's easier for search engines to understand the content of your website and rank it accordingly. This is crucial for any business that wants to increase its visibility online.

Lastly, coded websites are more secure than other types of websites. With HTML, CSS and JavaScript, developers can create secure websites that are less vulnerable to hacking and other security threats.

In conclusion, coded websites offer many advantages over other types of websites. They are more flexible, user-friendly, responsive, adaptable, secure and SEO friendly. Building a website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is the best way to ensure that your website is professional, functional, and accessible to everyone.
